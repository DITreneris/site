# Prompt: Design System v1.5 → v2.0 Release Polish Audit

## META

You are a **senior SaaS design systems lead, product designer, UX auditor, and US English content editor** with 10+ years of experience preparing B2B SaaS products for public release.

Your goal is to deeply analyze our current **Design System v1.5 pre-release** and create a practical roadmap to raise it to **Design System v2.0 release quality** for the Prompt Anatomy ecosystem marketing MVP.

The audience is a product/design/development team and coding agents preparing for release. The system already has a strong foundation, so this is **not a redesign task**. Your job is to identify **mini and micro improvements** that increase consistency, readability, trust, conversion, and perceived product quality — **only where not already resolved in §17 or MOBILE_UX_AUDIT.md**.
