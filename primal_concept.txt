# Prompt Anatomy Ecosystem

**Prompt Anatomy is an AI Operating System for modern teams.**

It helps organizations move from random prompting to structured, repeatable AI workflows — across learning, daily work, automation, marketing, HR, operations, and executive decision-making.

The ecosystem is built around one simple idea:

**AI should not be used only as a chat tool. It should become a structured work system.**

---

## 1. Main Platform

### **promptanatomy.app**

**The central AI operating system for teams and leaders.**

This is the main platform of the Prompt Anatomy ecosystem. It brings together structured training material, practical workflows, AI tools, and reusable templates for business teams.

It includes:

* **600+ templates and workflows**
* **60 best AI tools**
* **100-term AI glossary**
* Practical training material
* AI System OS logic for teams

**Best for:** CEOs, COOs, IT leaders, managers, and teams that want to implement AI in a structured way.

## 2. Enter

**Best for:** all staff, beginners, and teams that need a simple first step into AI.

# The Simple Ecosystem Logic

**Enter → Use → Create → Hire → Manage → Decide → Deepen → Play**

Each part of Prompt Anatomy has a clear role:

* **Enter** — learn the basics
* **Use** — apply AI in daily work
* **Create** — improve marketing and communication
* **Hire** — automate HR and recruitment
* **Manage** — run operations with AI
* **Decide** — scale business decisions
* **Deepen** — go deeper into AI systems, frameworks, and automation
* **Play** — practice in a structured sandbox before production workflows
