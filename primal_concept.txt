# Prompt Anatomy Ecosystem

**Prompt Anatomy is an AI Operating System for modern teams.**

6-module interactive training (6-block methodology) plus an ecosystem of focused prompt kits — from a free first lesson through executive playbooks.

The ecosystem is built around one simple idea:

**AI should not be used only as a chat tool. It should become a structured work system.**

---

## 1. Main Platform

### **promptanatomy.app**

**6-module training hub for teams and leaders.**

The core hub ships interactive training on the 6-block prompt methodology, plus checkout and lifetime access to the full course, tool catalog, and certificates.

It includes:

* **6-module interactive training (6-block methodology)**
* **15 quick-copy prompts in the training app**
* **31 evaluated AI tools (training catalog)**
* **Certificates after modules 3 and 6**

Aggregate ecosystem counts (600+ templates, 60 tools, 100-term glossary) span training plus subdomain kits — not one library on a single URL.

**Best for:** CEOs, COOs, IT leaders, managers, and teams that want structured AI training.

---

## 2. Enter

### **promptanatomy.cloud**

**Start with the first free lesson.**

A single interactive lesson and prompt library — the entry point before the full 6-module course.

**Best for:** all staff, beginners, and teams that need a simple first step into AI.

---

## 3. Use

### **promptanatomy.info**

**Organization prompt kit — 8 copy-ready prompts.**

Prompts for company profile, role clarity, KPIs, processes, and daily workflow structure.

**Best for:** operational teams, project managers, and analysts mapping how AI fits the organization.

---

## 4. Create

### **promptanatomy.space**

**Marketing prompt kit — 10 copy-ready prompts.**

Content calendars, LinkedIn posts, lead generation, PR, and brand-voice structures.

**Best for:** marketing, PR, communication, and content teams.

---

## 5. Hire

**HR hiring prompt kit — 10 prompts plus PDF guides.**

Sourcing, job descriptions, interviews, and HR communication workflows.

**Best for:** HR teams, recruiters, and people operations teams.

## 6. Manage

### **promptanatomy.ceo**

**CEO prompt generator and playbooks.**

**Best for:** CEOs, COOs, founders, and senior executives.

## 7. Decide

### **promptanatomy.pro**

## 8. Deepen

**Knowledge and deep-dive hub.**

# The Simple Ecosystem Logic

**Enter → Use → Create → Hire → Manage → Decide → Deepen → Play**

Each part of Prompt Anatomy has a clear role:

* **Enter** — first free lesson
* **Use** — organization prompt kit
* **Create** — marketing prompt kit
* **Hire** — HR hiring prompt kit
* **Manage** — CEO generator and playbooks
* **Decide** — executive prompt operating kit
* **Deepen** — knowledge hub
* **Play** — Corporate Ladder game

---

# Core Message

**Prompt Anatomy helps organizations turn AI from a random tool into a structured operating system.**

6-module training at the hub; focused prompt kits on each subdomain.

The result:

**Less random prompting. More structured execution.**
